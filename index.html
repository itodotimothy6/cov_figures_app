About Cov-Figures

Cov-Figures is a mobile app that allows you to track various statistics surrounding the COVID-19 pandemic in the US. This app allows you to monitor data all the way down to the county level. The statistics were crafted to what would be useful to anyone at this time, and this will not only allow you to observe your own county's info, but also look at what's happening in other US counties.
